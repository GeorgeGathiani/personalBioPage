George Gathiani

Radio Host, Poet, Rapper, Pan-Africanist

George Gathiani has his fingers dipped in all sorts of honey pots. A radio host, rapper, poet and media contributor, he is chipping away at the media block from as many angles as he can.

Originally from Nanyuki, Kenya, he is currently studying Communication Design in Washington University in St. Louis. A lover of language and entrepreneurship, he spends his free time working for start ups and adding French to the three languages he already speaks.
